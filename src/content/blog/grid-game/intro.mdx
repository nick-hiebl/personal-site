It's a puzzle format involving getting a grid into a desired state. You interact with it by clicking on the boxes to change their states.

<GridPuzzle
    isCentered
    schema={basicSchemaGenerator(1, 2, [1, 1], [2])}
    client:only
/>

You want to satisfy all of the rules indicated on the grid, to turn the whole board green. The only rule type we have so far is a number at the edge of a row or column, which indicates the total number of circles that should be present in that row or column.

<GridPuzzle
    isCentered
    schema={basicSchemaGenerator(2, 2, [1, 2], [2, 1])}
    client:only
/>

They can be of various sizes as you've seen so far, and can get increasingly complex.
